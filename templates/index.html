Our Services

Analysis

Our project offers assistance by carefully examining the uploaded data. The analysis is handled by Streamlit, a highly well-liked tool for data analysis and web rendering. The Streamlit is combined with the flask to achieve the finest outcomes.

Prediction

The K-Best automatic feature extraction is used by the browser during dynamic prediction to extract the best features. Logistic Regression, KNearest Classifier, and Decision Tree Classifier are the three models in use. As the basic model, we choose the model with the highest accuracy among these models.
